# Changelog

All notable changes to Sloth-Sales-Eido will be documented in this file.

## [v4.1] - 2026-04-17

### Codename: P3-Complete Edition (完整版)

Complete B2B sales intelligent assistant covering the full sales cycle from lead mining to win/loss review, with 22+ core capabilities.

### Added (7 new features on top of v3.1)
- **Win/Loss Review & Handover** — Win deals generate handover checklists; lost deals extract competitive intelligence
- **Customer News & Sentiment Monitoring** — Agent-Reach enhanced multi-channel scanning (LinkedIn, WeChat Official Accounts, Twitter, Weibo, etc.)
- **Benchmark Case Smart Matching** — Match best cases based on customer profile (industry, scale, pain points)
- **Industry Policy & Incentive Alerts** — Online search for industry support policies and incentives
- **Case Library Management** — Maintain and tag successful/failed case studies
- **Sales Team Leader Functions** — 18 team management capabilities including pipeline review, coaching, forecasting
- **Data Export Enhancement** — Full data export with customizable templates

### Inherited
- All 5 Core CRM features from v1.0
- All 4 Intelligent Enhancement features from v2.0
- All 7 Analysis & Competition features from v3.0
